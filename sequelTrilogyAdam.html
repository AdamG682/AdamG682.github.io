The Mandalorian Directed by Jon Favreau

This iteration of the Mighty Avengers was created by Brian Micheal Bendis and drawn by a collection of sketch artists. When looking at this series as a whole, I appreciate all that it does in expanding the lore of the Marvel Universe. I also enjoy how this series utilizes lesser-known characters and turns them into fan favorites.
