Hello, My Name is Adam Giles

Growing up my first introduction to the superhero and science fiction genre was by my Dad when he gave me some of his X-Men comic books to read for fun. Ever since then I have been invested in almost everything that revolves around superheroes and science fiction. I have lived in Oregon for the majority of my life, but I have also lived in other places such as Virgina, North Carolina, Florida, and even Puerto Rico.

This is my second year within the GIMM program at Boise State University and I have learned so much in my time here. I have learned a variety of coding languages such as HTML, JS, C#, and much more. I have also met some amazing people that I am proud to work.

For fun, I like to hike, draw, and hang out with friends. In regards to the more nerdy stuff, I am big on collecting figures and comic books while also being a huge gamer. Some of my favorite collectibles that I enjoy collecting are Funko Pops, I currently have around 350 Funko Pop figures. I also am a big fan in collecting comic books, I roughly have around 15 long boxes which equates to around 2,000 comic books give or take. I still am an avid collector of both and will continue to be.

I am currently playing through the Batman Arkham series again in anticipation of the new Batman movie. I also enjoy playing other games like Apex Legends, CS:GO, and Call of Duty, especially the Zombies game mode.
