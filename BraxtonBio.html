About Me

The Mandalorian takes place 5 years after the fall of the Galactic Empire and follows fellow bounty hunter, Din Djarin, who completes a variety of quests across the galaxy. The main premise of the show focuses on Din following one bounty that has him retrieve a child known as Grogu. After interacting with Grogu, Din goes against the bounty and decides to protect the child from other hunters and fighters of the remaining Imperial army. Throughout the fights and brawls with other characters in the Star Wars universe, Din Djarin realizes that protecting the child is the way.

This TV series is the most popular exclusive that the Disney+ streaming service has and is the very first live-action Star Wars television adaptation. Throughout this series, there are a lot of thematic elements that are reminiscent of that of the class Western, with the Mandolarion being the rough and tough cowboy. In this unique story, we run into a variety of characters that are new to audiences and some that audiences have seen in previous media. With the first two seasons of the show being wildly successful, another season is on the way.
