Our Personal Opinons on Star Wars

Thomas' Opinions

Although Kal Kestis is not apart of the Star Wars cinematic Universe, He is my favorite character because of the journey that he went through. You can meet Cal Kestis by playing, Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Oder, a game made by Respawn under EA. You play Cal and journey with your companion BD-1, a droid you find on the planet of Bogano. If you keep up with Star Wars you can see this droid in action during Boba Fett S1 EP5. Cal is a Jedi who had survived the elusive Order 66, executed by the Sith in conjunction with the clones. Roughly 5 years after the events of Revenge of the Sith, you are a Jedi in hiding when you are discovered by a group of Sith lords. On the run you find a group of Rebel soldiers who you work with to take down the new group of Sith to stop the spread of the Empire.

Braxton's Opinions

Anakin Skywalker was a legendary Force-sensitive human male who was a Jedi Knight of the Galactic Republic and the prophesied Chosen One of the Jedi Order, destined to bring balance to the Force. Also known as "Ani" during his childhood, Skywalker earned the moniker "Hero With No Fear" from his accomplishments in the Clone Wars. His alter ego, Darth Vader, the Dark Lord of the Sith, was created when Skywalker turned to the dark side of the Force, pledging his allegiance to the Sith Lord Darth Sidious at the end of the Republic Era.
