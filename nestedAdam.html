Future Projects In the Star Wars Universe


Obi-Wan Kenobi (May 25th, 2022)


Star Wars: Ahsoka (TBA)


Star Wars: The Acolyte (TBA)

Obi-Wan Kenobi, which is slated to release on Disney+ on May 25th, 2022, focuses on the events after Episode III and deals with the PTSD that the fellow Jedi Master is dealing with. This show takes heavy inspiration from the Star Wars Legends book titled Kenobi.


Ahsoka, which has an unknown release date on Disney+, is written by Dave Filoni and will focus on Ahsoka Tano's story as a Jedi. This show will continue off of her appearance in The Mandolarion, with possible flashbacks to her story after the events of Star Wars: Rebels.

What Adam is Excited for!

Star Wars: Eclipse

Eclipse is MMORPG styled video game that is developed by Quantic Dream and allows for players to express the story in a variety of narratives based on the player's actions. The timeline for this project shows that it takes place during the High Republic era and will also allow for players to interact on a variety of planets, but also within outer space. This game reminds me of Knights of the Old Republic, which I was a massive fan of as it expanded upon the lore of the Star Wars universe.

Star Wars: Andor

This television series focuses on telling us the life of the character, Andor. A Rebel fighter that we were introduced to in the successful Rogue One: A Star Wars Story. The show plans to establish more of a back story for Andor, as what we received in Rogue One was quite limited. The reason that I am so excited for this series is that before any footage or even any trailers, it already got confirmed to have a second season, meaning that the writers must have incredible faith in the show.

Ronin: A Visions Novel

Taking inspiration from the Star Wars: Visions episode of the same name, this novel focuses on establishing the lore behind the story and fleshing out what the episode introduced audiences to. I'm looking forward to this project as the ronin episode of Star Wars: Visions was quite intriguing with its storytelling and use of the Star Wars universe.

Star Wars: Rogue Squadron

This upcoming project, which is slated to release in 2023, focuses on a fleet of starfighter pilots flying across the galaxy completing a variety of missions that will help protect the lives of the innocent. This film is currently being directed by Patty Jenkins and is still in pre-production. I am quite excited for this project because nothing is as cool as epic space battles in Star Wars.

Rogue Squadron

The next Star Wars feature set for the big screen will make history as the first in the franchise to be helmed by a female director. Patty Jenkins (Wonder Woman) will usher in a "future era of the galaxy" with Rogue Squadron, introducing a new generation of Starfighter pilots as they earn their wings.


Obi-Wan Kenobi

The show is set 10 years after the events of Revenge of the Sith, where Kennedy says, Obi-Wan faced "his greatest defeat -- the downfall and corruption of his best friend and Jedi apprentice, Anakin Skywalker turned evil Sith Lord Darth Vader." Hayden Christensen will also return as Vader for "the rematch of the century."


Knights of the Old Republic remake

When it comes to acclaimed Star Wars games, you'll hear Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic's name mentioned more than just about anything else. A full remake is currently in the works from port specialist Aspyr, and it's headed to both PS5 and PC. EA's separate Star Wars: The Old Republic MMO is ongoing, but Knights of the Old Republic's story-focused single-player RPG style was riveting nearly two decades ago and should stand the test of time when the remake is ready to launch.


Ubisoft's Star Wars Project

Announced as EA's exclusivity period for the Star Wars franchise came to an end, Ubisoft's "The Star Wars Project," as it's currently known, is in development at Massive Entertainment--the same studio behind The Division and its sequel. Like those games, this Star Wars game will make use of the Snowdrop engine and will be set in an open world with a focus on narrative. Given that we have seen nothing of the game yet, it remains to be seen when it will actually release.

What Thomas is Excited for!

Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga

I've been excited for this game for so long. It was originally announced in 2019 and slated to release mid 2021 but got delayed due to the Covid-19 pandemic but is officaily releasing April 2022! This game remasters the classic Star Wars games I played as a child with up to date graphics as well as adding campaigns for the newer trilogy that released (7-9). It is the first open world Lego game that allows you to travel to different planets completing story missions that took place on said planet. As well as bringing back all the classic playable characters, they'll be adding the newer franchises to the game including Mando in his Beskar armor, classic character skins from the original games, and more.

Star Wars: Obi-Wan Kenobi

I remember hearing rumors about a possible Obi-Wan Kenobi movie or tv show and I couldn't even begin to imagine what they could do with it. Now that it's officially happening and the show is releasing May 2022, I've never been more excited then now, and we don't even know what the show is going to be about! All that is confirmed at the moment of writting this is that Hayden Christenson is returning to portray Darth Vader and Ray park is going to return as Darth maul! We have seen 2 renditions of Darth Maul so far, the one from the prequels and the one from the Clone Wars TV show. So I'm very excited to see how angry this version is compared to the two as well as how angry Darth Vader will be. Ewan Mcgregor did make a statement recently saying that Vader is scarier than what we saw in Rogue One, and if you saw that movie, he was pretty mencaing.
