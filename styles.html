The Phantom Menace was the first movie to release after the initial trilogy had released. Coming out in 1999, This was a very ambitious project for George Lucas to take on due to the scale of it. The Phantom Menace was the first movie to have full 100% CGI shot. The story follows Jedi Knights Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi who are tasked at stopping an invasion of droids on Naboo. Later on they travel to the planet of Naboo where they come in contact with a young boy named Anakin Skywalker.

Filled with some of the best choreography and visuals at the time, The Phantom Menace was met with very mixed reviews, most negative reviews pointed at the very controversial Jar Jar Binks. Jar Jar, a gungan became a notorious character in the star Wars community, some saying he's a good comic relief and others saying he was an annoyance. We were also introduced to the one of the greatest Star Wars scores, The Duel of Fates.
