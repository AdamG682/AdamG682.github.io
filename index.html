Episode I

THE BATTLE OF HTML


In a galactic world, students of GIMM 300 are presented with a task to build a website with HTML. The website must contain a variety of pages that fufill different objectives.
During this project, like-minded individuals, Thomas, Braxton, and Adam, team up to submit a HTML website focused on similar interests. Thomas will build his own webpage and another with numerous HTML tags. Braxton will focus on his own webpage and once again another one that utilizes a variety of attributes. Finally, Adam will create his own webpage, build an index with these credits, and another that has multiple nested layers. Their team will be called, The Placeholders.
Together, The Placeholders will use their combined knowledge to complete the numerous tasks and in the end submit their website.
